Whispers of the Dreamweaver

Whispers of the Dreamweaver draws inspiration from the shamanic traditions of the Mongolian steppe, where storytelling, spirit, and craftsmanship are deeply intertwined. This collection reimagines ancestral symbols and sacred rituals through a contemporary lens, weaving heritage into modern silhouettes.
